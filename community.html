We have initiated a club in our school community to promote future engagement in the technological industry. To keep this excitement of technology alive and to create additional enthusiasm for this industry, we have started a coding club. In our coding club, we cover the basics of coding and go all the way to web development. We have three categories: beginner, intermediate and advanced. We hope to help our club members achieve the best of their abilities and help them learn coding in an interesting way. Over 40 students have decided to join our coding club to enrich their knowledge of coding. Later in the year, we hope to start a robotics club. Our robotics club will integrate the skills and basic interpretation of coding into mechanical terms. This will help the students in our school deliver what they can imagine through the notion of technology.
