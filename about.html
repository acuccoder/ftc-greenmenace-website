Green Menace is a team of 13 students working hard to be successfull in the FIRST Competitions. Our team name, Green Menace, has an acronym, MENACE. It stands for:

  • Mechanical
  • Engineering [for]
  • Next-gen
  • Advanced
  • Cognitive
  • Explorer
We aspire to promote engagement in the technological industry, fostering innovation, collaboration, and hands-on learning.
